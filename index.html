About Akita Inu (AKITA)

The most unique Akita Inu (AKITA) on the Binance Smart Chain platform

A notable feature of AKITA is its contribution to charitable causes.

Part of the transaction fee is collected in the Akita Inu charity fund to support animal protection organizations and help abandoned pets. This helps to bring the AKITA community closer together and adds greater significance to its use.

Another unique feature of AKITA is its reduced transaction fee

When users trade with AKITA on the Binance Smart Chain, they receive a discount on the transaction fee compared to other coins on the same platform. This helps users save on transaction costs and gain a competitive advantage in the digital currency market.

In addition to being traded on digital currency exchanges, AKITA also has staking capabilities

Staking is a process where users can send their coins to a wallet and earn profits by holding them for a set period of time. This process helps to improve liquidity and increase the value of the coin, while also allowing users to earn profits without the need to buy or sell on the market.

Akita Inu (AKITA) has a unique feature of quarterly token burn.

Prior to being listed on digital currency exchanges, the total supply of AKITA is set at 500,000,000,000 AKITA


  • However, before listing, 25% equivalent to 150,000,000,000 AKITA will be burned, resulting in a real total supply of 350,000,000,000 AKITA. Afterwards, 10% of the total supply will be burned every quarter, gradually reducing the circulating amount of AKITA until only 10% (50,000,000,000 AKITA) of the total supply remains.
  • This mechanism helps to maintain the stability of AKITA's value and prevent inflation in the AKITA community.
